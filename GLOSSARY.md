# Glossary

All abbreviations used across the FCI-warehouse-MSPvsMSV reports, expanded and defined.
Grouped by theme. India-specific unless noted.

## Price, cost & market mechanisms

| Abbr. | Expansion | Note |
|---|---|---|
| **A2, A2+FL, C2** | CACP cost concepts | A2 = paid-out costs; +FL = family labour; C2 = comprehensive (incl. rent/interest) |
| **FRP** | Fair & Remunerative Price | statutory sugarcane price mills must pay |
| **MEP** | Minimum Export Price | export-floor price lever |
| **CMR** | Custom Milled Rice | millers grind government paddy for a fee, return rice to FCI |
| **CIF / FOB** | Cost-Insurance-Freight / Free On Board | landed vs port-side trade prices |
| **UV** | Unit Value | value ÷ quantity (implied price) |
| **PLC / ARC / LDP** | Price Loss Coverage / Agriculture Risk Coverage / Loan Deficiency Payment | US deficiency-payment instruments |
| **PEP / PEPRO / PGPM** | Prêmio para Escoamento de Produto / Prêmio Equalizador Pago ao Produtor / Política de Garantia de Preços Mínimos | Brazil price-support instruments |
| **CAP** | Common Agricultural Policy | EU farm-support framework |
| **CAP (storage)** | Cover And Plinth | open-air grain storage (context-dependent) |
| **AMS** | Aggregate Measurement of Support | WTO's trade-distorting-subsidy ceiling |
| **DBT** | Direct Benefit Transfer | cash-to-account subsidy delivery |
